We are Mayday PAC

The biggest challenge blocking fundamental reform is the belief that reform is not possible. We want to change that belief, by showing America that fundamental reform of the way campaigns are funded is absolutely possible.

Last year, more than 50,000 people rallied to the idea that change was possible. This year, we want to leverage that energy to close the gap between the number in Congress committed to reform and a majority.

And thus this platform: to help citizens to ask their leaders to step up and lead.

State of Reform

We are less than 70 votes from a majority in the House, and when the Senate bills are introduced, we’ll see how far from a majority we are in the Senate. But across the range of fundamental reform (identified on the site, RepsWith.US), we are tracking how close we are to a majority in Congress. Because the closer we get, the more likely it is that leaders from either party will make this issue central to the 2016 campaign.
